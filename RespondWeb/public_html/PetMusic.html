Pulcinella

Pulcinella was Domencio Scarlatti's cat. She spent alot of time with Scarlatti, frequently laying near him as he produced music. One day, she walked on his piano keys, hitting the notes that were at the start his piece Cat's Fugue

Mina

Mina was a dog owned by Sir Edward Elgar. Sir Elgar owned two other dogs which both heavily influenced his work. However, after the passing of his wife, Mina became the new center of his life.

Does music actually calm pets?

Yes

Like for humans, music can also influence pets. A article by the American Kennel Club found that dogs agitation levels do decrease from listening to music. specfiacly classical music. Classical music also encourages dogs to sleep and not bark. Other articles report the same affects for cats, reducing anxiety like with dogs. Though cats are more picky than dogs when it comes to music.
