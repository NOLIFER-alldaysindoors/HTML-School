Foxes

Foxes are intelligent, playful, and have been the subject of curiosity for centuries.

You Can Adopt Them!

Some organizations allow people to adopt or sponsor domesticated foxes.

Russian Domestication Experiment

In the 1950s, Soviet scientists began a selective breeding program to domesticate foxes.

Fox Pet Downsides and Upsides

Foxes are adorable but require special care. They can be affectionate but are not fully domesticated.
